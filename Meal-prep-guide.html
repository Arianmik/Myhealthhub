Essential Meal Prep Equipment

  • Glass meal prep containers (7 sets)
  • Quality chef's knife
  • Large sheet pans (2-3)
  • Instant Pot or slow cooker
  • Vegetable chopper (time saver)

Sample Meal Prep Menu

Meal Option 1 Option 2
Breakfast Overnight oats jars Veggie egg muffins
Lunch Quinoa salad bowls Lentil soup + whole grain bread
Dinner Sheet pan chicken & veggies Chickpea curry with rice
Snacks Cut veggies + hummus Greek yogurt + berries

Expert Tip

"Prep components, not full meals. Cook grains, roast veggies, and grill proteins separately. Mix and match during the week for variety without extra work." - Emily Wilson, Registered Dietitian

Time-Saving Prep Techniques

  • Chop all vegetables at once
  • Use frozen pre-cut vegetables
  • Cook grains in bulk (quinoa, rice)
  • Roast multiple sheet pans simultaneously
  • Use slow cooker for hands-off cooking
