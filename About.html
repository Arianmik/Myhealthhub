About HealthHub

Our Mission

At HealthHub, our mission is to provide evidence-based health information that empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their well-being. We believe that accurate, accessible health information is a fundamental right.

Our Team

Our content is created and reviewed by a team of healthcare professionals including:

  • Dr. Sarah Johnson - MD, Board Certified in Internal Medicine
  • Michael Chen - Registered Dietitian and Nutrition Specialist
  • Dr. Amanda Rodriguez - PhD in Psychology and Mental Health Counselor
  • James Wilson - Certified Personal Trainer and Fitness Coach

With over 50 years of combined experience in healthcare, our team ensures that all content meets the highest standards of medical accuracy.

Our Editorial Process

Every article on HealthHub goes through a rigorous editorial process:

  1. Research: Our writers research using peer-reviewed journals and reputable sources
  2. Writing: Content is drafted by subject matter experts
  3. Review: Medical professionals verify all health claims
  4. Updating: We regularly review and update content as new research emerges

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Providing free access to essential health information
  • Maintaining editorial independence
  • Disclosing any potential conflicts of interest
  • Correcting errors promptly and transparently
